Tom Creed is an architect

in practice for over 25 years.

In that time his practice has been invested in various projects ranging from one-off houses, warehouses and interior fit outs. His practice has always been about diversification, learning and trying to produce environmentally aware design that ‘solves, simply’ rather creating complication and confusion. More recently, his practice has also extended to furniture, product design and technology for architecture. The app that he designed (Build Regs) provides easy access to all building regulations and connects builders with suppliers. The app is currently available to download from the Irish and UK iTunes store.
